# today-md

### Plan your day in Markdown.

A native macOS task planner with a Kanban board, quick capture, and Markdown notes — all stored locally on your Mac.

---

## Why today-md?

Most task managers try to do everything. today-md does one thing well: help you decide what to work on **today**.

Organize tasks across three lanes — **Today**, **This Week**, and **Backlog** — then focus. Write rich notes in Markdown. Search everything instantly. No account required, no cloud dependency, no subscription.

---

## Features

### Kanban Board
Three lanes keep your priorities clear. Drag tasks between Today, This Week, and Backlog. Collapse lanes you don't need. Hit **Focus Today** to zero in on what matters right now.

### Markdown Notes
Every task has a full Markdown editor with headings, bold, italic, strikethrough, code blocks, checklists, and more. Notes are automatically archived as `.md` files on disk.

### Quick Capture
Hover your mouse at the top of the screen to reveal the **Dynamic Island** — a floating panel for adding tasks without breaking your flow.

### Menu Bar
See today's tasks at a glance from the menu bar. Add new tasks without opening the main window.

### Custom Lists
Create lists with names, colors, and icons. Eight color themes and 20+ SF Symbols to choose from.

### Subtasks & Checklists
Break tasks into subtasks with checkboxes. Track progress visually.

### Full-Text Search
Instantly search across task titles, notes, and subtasks — powered by SQLite FTS.

### Import & Export
Export tasks as JSON backups or Markdown files. Import backups with merge or replace. Optionally sync via iCloud Drive or any cloud folder.

### Local-First
All data lives on your Mac in a local SQLite database. No analytics, no telemetry, fully offline.

## Get Started

**Download the latest release:**

```
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/arthurliebhardt/today-md/main/scripts/install.sh | bash
```

Or grab the `.zip` from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/arthurliebhardt/today-md/releases).

Requires **macOS 14.0** or later.
